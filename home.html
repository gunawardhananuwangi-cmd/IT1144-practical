he University of Vavuniya is a prominent public state university located in Pampaimadu, Vavuniya, Sri Lanka. Originally established in 1991 as the Northern Province Affiliated University College and later functioning as a campus under the University of Jaffna, it was officially elevated to a fully independent, standalone national university on August 1, 2021, making it the 17th state university in Sri Lanka.Core Academic FacultiesThe university hosts over 3,000 students across its 10 academic departments and offers 14-degree programs. Its primary operations are divided into three core faculties:Faculty of Applied Science: Focuses on advancing technical knowledge through disciplines such as Bio-science, Physical Science, and Environmental Technology.Faculty of Business Studies: Specializes in management, finance, marketing, economics, and project management.Faculty of Technological Studies: Houses practical and rapidly expanding fields like Information and Communication Technology (ICT) and Information Technology.
